#### Implicit type casting

`str + num` - *concatenation*  
`str - | * | / num` - *tries cast str to num*


#### Simple methods

`document.write(), document.writeln()`  
`alert(), prompt(), confirm()` - *blocks script*  
`parseInt(), parseFloat()`

#### Simple operators

`+ - * /`  
`%` - mod  
`result = +str` - `unary +` *casts str to num*
